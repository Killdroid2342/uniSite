ImpactSBCC is a creative club founded by Mohamed Al-Shaibi to bring more creativity and collaboration to campus life. Wanting students to have a space to try new ideas and make things together, he began building events that mix art, fun, and community. Co-Founder Cecelia Moscatelli was able to support the project by helping shape the vision and adding her skills to make each event come to life. Together, they've made ImpactSBCC a hub for activities like bead jewelry-making, tote bag painting, and canvas roulette giving students not just a club to join, but a place to create, connect, and leave their mark on SBCC.
