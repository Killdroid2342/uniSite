Impact SBCC is a creative club created for students who want to make crafty, ingenious, fun things together. Throughout the semester, our events bring people to create, explore, and experiment in a variety of ways. Through Bead Jewelry-Making Labs, which drop to Painting Tote Bags and Canvas Roulette, every event lets students create something bold, unexpected, and totally original.

We’re not just a club, we’re moving ahead to return creativity to campus life. Act more and lecture less. Fewer rules, more ideas.

If you like getting your hands dirty and your mind moving, you belong here.
