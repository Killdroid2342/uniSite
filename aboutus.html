ImpactSBCC is a creative club built for students who want to make crafty, ingenious, fun things together. Throughout the semester, our events bring people to create, explore, and experiment in a variety of ways. We commence with Bead Jewelry-Making Labs where students can design wearable pieces that reflect their individual style to painting tote bags that showcase personal flair to our interactive Canvas Roulette sessions where art evolves with every new touch, our events are all about bold, unexpected creation.

We’re not just a club, we’re moving ahead to return creativity to campus life. Act more and lecture less. Fewer rules, more ideas.

If you like getting your hands dirty and your mind moving, you belong here.
